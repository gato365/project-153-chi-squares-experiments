Experiment 5 — Birth Month Distribution

If births were spread equally, each month would contain ~8.3% of births. Tap your birth month and we'll test whether our class matches the uniform expectation.

☕ Experiment 6 — Where You're From & What You Drink

The question: Is the kind of coffee drink you prefer associated with whether you grew up on the coast or inland? Some say coastal folks lean toward fancy lattes and cafe culture, while inland folks prefer simpler black coffee. Let's test it with a chi-square test of independence on a 2×4 table.

Instructions

1

Pick where you grew up: coast (within ~50 miles of an ocean) or inland.

2

Pick your usual coffee drink. If you don't drink coffee, pick "none / tea."

3

Tap submit.
